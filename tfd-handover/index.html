How the audience is segmented

Ads and copy are built by core motivation, not demographics. Five segments.
The Precision Perfectionist · ~25% · driver: mastery
High-achievers who won through discipline and are frustrated their body won't respond to the usual effort. Skews female, ~69.
The Medical Refugee · ~30% · driver: vindication
Chronic-pain sufferers told "nothing more we can do." Bitter at the system, TFD is the last resort.
The Defiant Competitor · ~20% · driver: defiance
Former athletes who refuse to slow down and keep getting injured on outdated methods. Skews male.
The Independence Guardian · ~15% · driver: autonomy
Often widowed or living alone. Deeply fear becoming a burden; use fitness to stay self-reliant.
The Executive Optimizer · ~10% · driver: performance
Still-working professionals, can't show weakness, need ROI and a schedule that fits.
